October 23, 2025 | Topic: CSS Layout

Why I Finally Understand `space-between` (It's a Game-Changer!)

I had a major CSS breakthrough today! I was struggling with my navigation bar until I truly figured out how **Flexbox**'s `justify-content: space-between` works. Seriously, it's a huge difference from `space-around`. My Photo Gallery project now looks way cleaner. It only took 4 hours of debugging, lol.

October 15, 2025 | Topic: JavaScript Debugging

Debugging: My New Best Friend is `console.log()`

I wasted a whole afternoon on my calculator app because I mixed up two variable names. Two hours! I realized that debugging isn't just about fixing code, it's about being a detective. Now I put **`console.log()`** everywhere. It's brutal when things break, but I actually felt amazing when I found that tiny mistake.
